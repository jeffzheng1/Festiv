W&W

Wardt van der Harst & Willem Van Hanegem met during a chance encounter at Trance Energy 2007. Willem and Wardt initially started out producing their own respective songs with tracks like ‘Ensure’, ‘Show my Shuffle’, ‘Origin’ & ‘Stab My Speaker’. Their serendipitous Trance Energy meet quickly resulted in their debut W&W production ‘Mustang’. ‘Dome’ & ‘Arena’ (which featured on the Armin Only DVD) followed up its freshman success and fast found the CDJs of trance luminaries like Tiësto, Ferry Corsten, Markus Schulz, Sander Van Doorn & Judge Jules. Tipped by Armin van Buuren as “the best up-and-coming production duo out there”, W&W then produced ‘Mainstage’, the Sean Tyas-remixed ‘D.N.A’ and their Ummet Ozcan collaboration ‘Synergy’. Their remixes have proved equally popular – most notably on Armin’s ‘Rain’, ‘Remedy’ (by UK pop starlet Little Boots) and those of Sander van Doorn, Leon Bolier, Sied van Riel, & others.

Trance Energy 2008 featured Sander van Doorn playing W&W's ‘Mustang’. Galvanized by its floor-response (and defining their sounds as “big room trance with a techy edge”), W&W took up DJ-ing. Their gigs include Poland’s Sunrise Festival, the A State Of Trance 400 gigs and a spot playing alongside Tiësto at in Kiev. Just two years further down the road from their first gigs together, they were featured in Trance Energy 2010.
