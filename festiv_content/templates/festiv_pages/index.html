What is Festiv?

Festiv is a new way to experience music, using SoundCloud to generate the best EDM sets for you to listen to with just one click. Listen to the Main Stage if you're looking to listen to the widest spectrum of dance music or choose genre sub stages if you have something more specific in mind. You can also search for specific artists or browse through the Festiv lineup. Spend less time searching for music and more time enjoying it.

Festivals & Concerts

The biggest music festivals of 2014.

  • Coachella
    April 11 - 13 & April 18 - 20

    Coachella is a two-weekend, three-day music and arts festival held at the Empire Polo Club in Indio, California.

  • Tomorrowland
    July 18 - 20 & July 25 - 27

    Tomorrowland is a large electronic music festival held in Belgium.

  • Creamsfields
    August 22 - 24

    Creamfields is a large British dance music festival featuring DJs and live acts in Daresbury, England.

  • Stereosonic
    November 29 - 30 & December 5 -7

    Stereosonic is an annual music festival held in Australia in November and early December. Stereosonic is held in Brisbane, Adelaide, Perth, Sydney, and Melbourne.

  • Ultra Music Festival Miami
    March 28 - 31

    Ultra Music Festival (UMF) is an annual outdoor electronic music festival that occurs in March in the city of Miami, Florida, United States.

  • EDC Las Vegas
    June 20 - 22

    Electric Daisy Carnival in Las Vegas is the world's largest electronic music festival.
